Welcome to Verdant

Founded in 2018 by acclaimed chef Michael Richardson, Verdant represents our commitment to sustainable fine dining that honors both tradition and innovation.

We source our ingredients from local organic farms, ensuring that each dish not only delights your palate but also supports our community and environment.

Our culinary team crafts seasonal menus that showcase the finest ingredients at their peak, transforming them into unforgettable dining experiences that engage all your senses.
